<p><p>The wireless speaker market is primarily categorized into three technologies: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Infrared. Bluetooth technology offers convenience with short-range connectivity, ideal for personal use. Wi-Fi technology enables high-quality audio streaming over longer distances, suitable for home setups and smart systems. Infrared technology, while less common, provides a reliable connection in localized environments but requires unobstructed line-of-sight. Each type caters to different user needs, from portability and ease of use to sound quality and multi-room audio capabilities.</p></p>
