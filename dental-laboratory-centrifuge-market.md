<p><p>The Dental Laboratory Centrifuge market comprises three main types: low-speed, high-speed, and ultra-speed centrifuges. Low-speed dental centrifuges operate at lower RPMs, suitable for applications like sedimentation of dental materials. High-speed centrifuges offer faster separation, ideal for dental labs requiring efficient processing of samples like blood or saliva. Ultra-speed centrifuges provide even greater RPMs, enabling rapid separation of complex compounds and enhancing the precision and quality of dental restorations or diagnostics. Each type caters to specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
