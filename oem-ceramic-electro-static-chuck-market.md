<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OEM Ceramic Electro Static Chuck market is primarily segmented into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. The Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces to hold wafers securely, offering high stability and precision. In contrast, the JR Type employs a combination of an insulating layer and a conductive substrate, enhancing holding force at lower voltages. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, providing precise wafer handling for processes such as photolithography and etching, thereby improving overall production efficiency.</p></p>
