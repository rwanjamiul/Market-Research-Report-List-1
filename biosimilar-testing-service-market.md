<p><p>The Biosimilar Testing Service Market encompasses several types, including Pharmacokinetic Assay, Tiered Immunogenicity Testing, and Others. Pharmacokinetic Assays evaluate the absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion of biosimilars, ensuring they behave similarly to reference biologics in the body. Tiered Immunogenicity Testing assesses the immune response generated by biosimilars, identifying potential adverse reactions. Other services may include stability testing and analytics for quality assurance. Together, these testing methodologies ensure the safety, efficacy, and regulatory compliance of biosimilars.</p></p>
