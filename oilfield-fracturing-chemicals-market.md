<p><p>The oilfield fracturing chemicals market comprises several key types, each serving specific functions. Clear brine fluids enhance well stability and prevent fluid loss. Shale inhibitors protect shale formations from swelling. Lubricants facilitate smoother movement during drilling, while non-emulsifiers prevent the mixing of oil and water phases. H2S scavengers neutralize harmful hydrogen sulfide gas, ensuring safety. Defoamers eliminate unwanted foam that can hinder operations, and surfactants improve fluid flow and enhance recovery efficiency, making them essential for effective fracturing processes.</p></p>
