<p><p>Sorters in logistics are essential systems for efficient order fulfillment and inventory management. Flat sorters utilize a single plane for sorting items, ideal for small, flat packages. Cross belt sorters operate with belts that transport items across direction changes, suitable for diverse product shapes. Tilt tray sorters feature trays that tilt to discharge items at specific points, optimizing sorting accuracy. Other types include pocket sorters and overhead sorters, each designed to meet unique operational needs in various warehouse environments.</p></p>
