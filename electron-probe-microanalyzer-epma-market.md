<p><p>The Electron Probe Microanalyzer (EPMA) is a sophisticated analytical tool used primarily for the detailed analysis of the composition and structure of materials at the micro-scale. It employs a finely focused electron beam to excite the sample, allowing for precise measurements of elemental composition through the detection of characteristic X-rays emitted from the sample. EPMA is widely utilized in various industries, including materials science, geology, and semiconductor manufacturing, due to its ability to provide high-resolution imaging and quantitative analysis.</p><p>The EPMA market is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing demand for advanced materials characterization in research and industrial applications.
