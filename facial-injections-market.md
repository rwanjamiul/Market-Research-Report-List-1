<p><p>The facial injections market consists of several types, each catering to different aesthetic needs. Hyaluronic acid wrinkle fillers are popular for their ability to retain moisture and provide volume, offering temporary results. Synthetic wrinkle fillers, such as poly-L-lactic acid, stimulate collagen production for longer-lasting effects. Collagen wrinkle fillers, derived from animal sources, enhance skin elasticity but have largely been replaced by newer options. Autologous wrinkle fillers, made from a patient's own fat or cells, provide a natural look with minimal risk of rejection.</p></p>
