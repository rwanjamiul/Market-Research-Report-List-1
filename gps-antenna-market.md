<p><strong>The GPS Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Polarization</li><li>Circular Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna market is divided primarily into two types based on polarization: vertical polarization and circular polarization. Vertical polarization antennas are designed to receive signals that are oriented vertically, making them suitable for specific applications like land-based tracking. In contrast, circular polarization antennas can receive signals from multiple angles, enhancing reliability in dynamic environments like aviation and maritime navigation. The choice between these types depends on the application requirements and environmental considerations, influencing market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
