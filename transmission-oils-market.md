<p><strong>The Transmission Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Transmission Fluid</li><li>Manual Transmission Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission oils market primarily consists of two types: Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) and Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF). ATF is designed for automatic transmissions, providing necessary lubrication, cooling, and cleaning, while also enabling smooth shifting. MTF, on the other hand, is formulated for manual transmissions, focusing on reducing friction and wear, allowing for optimal gear engagement. These fluids are essential in ensuring the efficient operation and longevity of different transmission systems in vehicles, catering to specific mechanical needs.</p></p>
