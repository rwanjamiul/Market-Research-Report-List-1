<p><ul><li>Point of Entry</li><li>Point of Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential water treatment devices market comprises two primary applications: Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU). POE systems are installed at the main water supply, treating water for the entire home, ensuring comprehensive purification and reducing contaminants before distribution. In contrast, POU devices are installed at specific outlets, such as faucets or under sinks, providing targeted purification for drinking and cooking water. Both applications enhance water quality, addressing concerns about contaminants, taste, and safety in residential settings.</p></p>
