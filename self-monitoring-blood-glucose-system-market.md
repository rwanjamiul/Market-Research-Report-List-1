<p><p>The Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose System market comprises two primary types: Blood Glucose Meters and Blood Glucose Test Strips. Blood Glucose Meters are electronic devices that measure blood glucose levels using a small blood sample, providing timely feedback for diabetes management. Blood Glucose Test Strips are disposable components used in conjunction with meters; they react with the blood sample to produce a glucose reading. Together, these tools empower individuals to monitor their blood sugar levels effectively for optimal health management.</p></p>
