<p><p>Hotel management WiFi software can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud. On-premises solutions are installed directly on a hotel's local servers, offering greater control and customization, but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely, allowing hotels to access the software via the internet. These offer scalability, lower initial costs, and automated updates, making them suitable for hotels seeking flexibility and easy management of their WiFi infrastructure.</p></p>
