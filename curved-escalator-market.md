<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Curved Escalator market encompasses two primary types: Isokinetic Operation and Frequency Conversion. Isokinetic Operation maintains a constant speed regardless of incline, ensuring smooth transit and efficiency. In contrast, Frequency Conversion allows for adjustable speeds based on demand, optimizing energy use and user experience. Both technologies enhance passenger flow and safety, adapting to various architectural needs. Together, they represent advancements in escalator design, improving mobility solutions in complex environments while addressing energy efficiency and operational performance.</p></p>
