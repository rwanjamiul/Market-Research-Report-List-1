<p><p>The Vehicle Boom Barrier Gate market is primarily categorized into two types: straight and crank. Straight boom barriers feature a linear movement, collapsing horizontally to restrict vehicle entry and exit efficiently. They are ideal for locations with ample space. In contrast, crank boom barriers utilize a pivoting mechanism that allows the boom to rise and lower, making them suitable for tighter spaces. Both types enhance traffic management and security in various applications, from toll plazas to parking facilities.</p></p>
