<p><p>Compact filters are designed to capture airborne particles with varying efficiencies based on their size, categorized into ISO ePM1, ISO ePM2.5, and ISO ePM10. The ISO ePM1 market targets ultrafine particles (≤1 micron), ideal for environments needing high air quality, such as hospitals. ISO ePM2.5 focuses on fine particles (≤2.5 microns), balancing performance and energy consumption, suited for residential and commercial buildings. The ISO ePM10 market addresses larger particulate matter (≤10 microns), commonly utilized in industrial applications and air handling systems.</p></p>
