<p><p>The Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (LFRT) market encompasses two key types: LFT-G (Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic-Glass) and LFT-D (Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic-Duplex). LFT-G utilizes glass fibers to enhance mechanical strength and stiffness, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, LFT-D combines two types of fibers, often improving impact resistance and overall performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for lightweight, durable materials in industries seeking enhanced performance and sustainability.</p></p>
