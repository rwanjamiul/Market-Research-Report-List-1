<p><ul><li>Automatic Reset</li><li>Manual Reset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Undercurrent relay market segments include Automatic Reset and Manual Reset types. Automatic Reset relays restore their original state once the fault is cleared, enabling a quick recovery without manual intervention. This type is often preferred for applications requiring continuous operation. Conversely, Manual Reset relays require operator intervention to reset after a fault, providing a safeguard against unintentional reactivation, which can be crucial in sensitive systems. Both types ensure safety and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
