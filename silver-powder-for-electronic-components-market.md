Flake silver powder consists of thin, flat particles suitable for applications requiring high conductivity and thermal management, often used in coatings and inks. In contrast, spherical silver powder comprises round particles that enhance flowability and packing density, making it ideal for applications like 3D printing and paste formulations. Both types play crucial roles in the electronics industry due to their superior properties.</p></p>
