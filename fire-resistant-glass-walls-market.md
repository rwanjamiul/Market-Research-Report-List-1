<p><p>The fire-resistant glass walls market is segmented into single-layer and double-layer technology. Single-layer fire-resistant glass walls consist of one solid pane designed to withstand heat and flames, making them suitable for low-risk environments. In contrast, double-layer fire-resistant glass walls feature two panes separated by an insulating air gap, offering enhanced thermal performance and improved fire ratings. This makes double-layer options ideal for higher-risk areas where fire safety regulations demand greater protection and durability, ensuring user safety and compliance.</p></p>
