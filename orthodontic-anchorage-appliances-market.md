<p><p>The orthodontic anchorage appliances market comprises various types, primarily including bands and buccal tubes, alongside miniscrews. Bands are fixed metal rings cemented to molars, serving as stable anchorage points for orthodontic appliances. Buccal tubes are attached to bands facilitating the connection of archwires. Miniscrews, or temporary anchorage devices, are small titanium screws placed in the jawbone, offering robust skeletal anchorage for tooth movement. These components are essential for effective orthodontic treatment, ensuring precise alignment and control of dental positioning.</p></p>
