<p><p>The duodenal endoscope market is categorized into two main types: fiber duodenal endoscopes and electronic duodenal endoscopes. Fiber duodenal endoscopes utilize flexible fiber optics to transmit light and images, offering a lightweight and maneuverable option for gastrointestinal examinations. In contrast, electronic duodenal endoscopes employ advanced electronic imaging technology, providing superior image quality, enhanced visualization, and additional functionalities such as video recording. Both types serve critical roles in diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the duodenum and surrounding areas.</p></p>
