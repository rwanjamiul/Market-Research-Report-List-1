<p><p>Air Conditioning Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are critical components that regulate refrigerant flow in HVAC systems, improving efficiency and performance. The EEV market is primarily divided into two types: electromagnetic EEVs and electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs use a coil to control the valve's opening and closing, providing precise flow control. In contrast, electric EEVs employ a stepper motor for enhanced accuracy and adaptability across varying operating conditions, making them suitable for modern, energy-efficient cooling applications.</p></p>
