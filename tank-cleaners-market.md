<p><p>The tank cleaners market is primarily divided into two types: neutral and alkaline cleaners. Neutral cleaners are pH-balanced formulations effective for general cleaning tasks without causing damage to surfaces or equipment. They are ideal for sensitive materials and routine maintenance. Alkaline cleaners, on the other hand, have a higher pH level, making them effective in breaking down heavy soil, grease, and organic residues. They are commonly used in industries requiring deep cleaning, such as food and beverage, where sanitation is critical.</p></p>
