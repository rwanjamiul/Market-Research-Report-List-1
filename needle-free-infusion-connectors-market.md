<p><strong>The Needle-free Infusion Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Non Positive Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle-free infusion connectors are classified into positive pressure and non-positive pressure types based on their functionality in maintaining catheter patency. Positive pressure connectors create a seal that prevents blood reflux and reduces the risk of thrombus formation, ensuring optimal flow during infusions. In contrast, non-positive pressure connectors allow for passive fluid flow and rely on gravity or other mechanisms. Both types are essential in enhancing safety and reducing infection risk during intravenous therapy in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
