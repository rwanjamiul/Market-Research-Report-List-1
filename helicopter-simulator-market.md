<p><strong>The Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS and FTD</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helicopter simulator market comprises three main types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS), Flight Training Devices (FTD), and Aviation Training Devices (ATD). FFS are advanced simulators providing realistic flight experiences with motion capabilities and detailed visual systems. FTD offer a less immersive experience, focusing on specific flight scenarios without full motion, while ATDs are primarily used for procedural and systems training without requiring full aircraft dynamics. These types cater to different training needs and budgets within the aviation industry.</p></p>
