<p><p>The interventional catheters market encompasses various devices essential for cardiovascular procedures. Coronary guide wires aid in navigating arteries during interventions. Balloon dilatation catheters are used to widen narrowed vessels, while guiding catheters facilitate the placement of other devices. Coronary stents provide structural support to maintain vessel openness, and vascular closure devices (VCDs) ensure secure closure of access sites post-procedure. Together, these products enhance the efficacy and safety of minimally invasive cardiovascular treatments.</p></p>
