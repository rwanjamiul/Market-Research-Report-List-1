<p><ul><li>Horizontal Impact Mills</li><li>Vertical Impact Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impact mills are crucial in size reduction processes and exhibit two primary types: horizontal and vertical impact mills. Horizontal impact mills utilize a horizontal rotor to accelerate particles against a chamber wall, enabling efficient grinding of materials like minerals and food products. In contrast, vertical impact mills feature a vertical rotor that achieves similar outcomes but with different airflow dynamics, enhancing separation and particle size control. Both types are integral to various industries, including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
