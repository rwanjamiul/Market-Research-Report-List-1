<p><ul><li>Low Strength Level</li><li>Medium Strength Level</li><li>High Strength Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threadlocker fluid is categorized into low, medium, and high strength levels based on bonding strength and application suitability. Low-strength threadlockers allow for easy disassembly and are ideal for smaller fasteners or components exposed to light vibrations. Medium-strength options provide a balance between secure adhesion and disassembly ease, suitable for most standard applications. High-strength threadlockers offer maximum adhesion, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications where permanent locking is needed, often requiring heat or tools for removal.</p></p>
