<p><p>The medical respiratory mask market features various types, including Positive Pressure Air Respirators and Long Tube Respirators. Positive Pressure Air Respirators supply clean, pressurized air to the user, ensuring a secure fit and effective filtration, making them ideal for high-risk environments. Long Tube Respirators, on the other hand, connect to a centralized air supply, allowing for extended use and mobility while maintaining adequate respiratory protection. Both types are essential for healthcare settings to protect against airborne contaminants and enhance patient care.</p></p>
