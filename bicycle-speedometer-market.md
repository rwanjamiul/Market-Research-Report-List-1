<p><p>The bicycle speedometer market is categorized into three main types: Wired Computers, Wireless Computers, and Wireless & GPS Computers. Wired Computers connect directly to the bike with cables, providing reliable data but limited placement flexibility. Wireless Computers use radio signals to transmit data, offering easier installation and cleaner aesthetics, though they may face interference issues. Wireless & GPS Computers combine advanced tracking features with GPS technology, providing detailed data on speed, distance, and location, appealing to tech-savvy cyclists seeking enhanced performance insights.</p></p>
