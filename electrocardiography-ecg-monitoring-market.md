<p><strong>The Electrocardiography (ECG) Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holter Monitoring</li><li>Event Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrocardiography (ECG) monitoring encompasses various types, primarily Holter monitoring and event monitoring. Holter monitoring involves continuous ECG recording over 24 to 48 hours, capturing heart rhythms during daily activities to diagnose irregularities. In contrast, event monitoring allows patients to record their heart's electrical activity only when experiencing symptoms, using portable devices. Both methods provide critical data for diagnosing cardiac conditions, aiding in personalized treatment planning and improving patient outcomes in cardiovascular health management.</p></p>
